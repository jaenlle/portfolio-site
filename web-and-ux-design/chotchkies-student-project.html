Chotchkie's Restaurant Student Project

For this student project I was assigned to pick a fictional restaurant and design a site that captured its feeling from the film or television show in which it is featured. I chose Chotchkie's, a T.G.I. Friday's-style restaurant from the iconic film Office Space. Per the assignment the site had to feature a full menu, contact page, and promotions as well as be fully responsive. The site was also required to use elements of Javascript and jQuery.
