Samsonite - Take It Out On A Samsonite (Student Work)

Challenge

Bring an image of strength to the forefront of peoples' minds when they think of Samsonite luggage.

Solution

Use real people, at peak travel times when delays and cancellations are most common, to show the world that Samsonite luggage can take anything travel can dish out, even the frustrations of a disgruntled traveler.
