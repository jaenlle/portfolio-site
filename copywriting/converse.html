Converse - The Creative's Canvas (Personal Project)

Challenge

Establish items Chuck Taylor All-Stars as the go-to shoe for creative people.

Solution

Combine online shoe customization with an interactive experience that goes beyond mere aesthetics and into the core emotions that drive the world's creative community. Every custom design becomes interwoven with other design in the Converse virtual "canvas".
