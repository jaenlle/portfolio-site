IWF Construction & Glazing

This site was built as part of a class project during the UF master's degree in Web Design & Online Communication program. IWF Construction & Glazing is a small general contractor business based in Miami, FL. I began the process of building the site by researching competing companies of various sizes and their websites, then built wireframes and mockups before beginning work. Initially a static site, I recently converted it to a fully-responsive site. Further changes were made after usability testing which included one-on-one interviews. The site includes both slider and responsive menu jQuery plugins.
